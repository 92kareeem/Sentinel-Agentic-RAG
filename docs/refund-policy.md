# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. The product must be unused and in its
original packaging. Digital goods are refundable only if the license key was never activated.
Subscriptions cancelled mid-cycle receive a prorated refund for the unused period.

## Fees and Processing

| Region | Restocking fee | Processing days | Method          |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| US     | 5%             | 3               | Original card   |
| EU     | 0%             | 5               | Original card   |
| India  | 2%             | 7               | Bank transfer   |

Restocking fees are waived entirely for defective items and for warranty claims filed within
the first 14 days.

## Escalation
If a refund is not received within the stated processing window, customers should contact
support with their order id. Escalations unresolved after 10 business days are routed to the
regional operations lead, who has authority to issue manual refunds up to $500 without
additional approval.
