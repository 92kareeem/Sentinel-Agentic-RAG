# Employee Onboarding Guide

## First Week
New hires receive laptop and credentials on day one. Security training (module SEC-101) must be
completed within the first five business days. The buddy program pairs each new hire with a
tenured teammate for the first month.

## Access Requests
System access follows least privilege. Requests go through the ACCESS-REQ form and require
manager approval. Production database access additionally requires the on-call certification
exam (code OPS-204) with a passing score of 80%.

## Equipment Allowances

| Role        | Laptop budget | Monitor | Refresh cycle |
|-------------|---------------|---------|---------------|
| Engineer    | $2,400        | Dual    | 3 years       |
| Designer    | $2,800        | Single 5K | 3 years     |
| Sales       | $1,600        | Single  | 4 years       |

Unused allowance does not roll over between refresh cycles.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week after their probation period ends.
Fully remote arrangements require VP approval and are reviewed annually each January.
